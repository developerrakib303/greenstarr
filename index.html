Our Workflow

The Green Star Standard

A rigorous 10-step quality protocol designed to restore your air conditioning unit to factory-fresh performance. Every step verified.

01
Clean Filters & Panel

The first steps of the servicing start from the outer parts. The unit cover will be removed, and the air filter will be cleaned thoroughly to remove dust accumulation.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
02
Remove Water Tray

The water tray functions to gather water before it flows to the drainage pipe. All the dust, jelly, dirt and other debris stuck inside will be cleaned and vacuumed.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
03
Clean Indoor Cooling Coil

The fan coil unit (FCU) will be vacuumed properly in an up-down motion and repeatedly to ensure no dust spots are missed, ensuring optimal airflow.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
04
Flush Drainage Tray

The vacuum will be left attached for around 1-2 minutes at the end of the drainage pipe to clear any clogged dirt or jelly inside, preventing future leaks.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
05
Check Outdoor Coil

The technician will check if all the fan coils are working properly by visually inspecting and checking for proper condensation on the outdoor unit.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
06
Check Refrigerant Level

We then double-check the outdoor condenser unit to make sure that there is still enough gas. If necessary, compressor pressure will be assessed.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
07
Clean Evaporator Coil

The evaporator coil will be checked, followed by vacuuming and brushing the blower wheel to ensure nothing hinders smooth rotation.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
08
Reattach Water Tray

After all cleaning steps are done, the water tray will be reattached. We ensure it clips on properly and fully to prevent future leaks.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
09
Tighten Components

We double-check and tighten all electrical components and fan bearings to ensure there are no loose parts or rattling noises.

QUALITY CHECK POINT
10
Final Wipe & Reassembly

Wipe down, clean any leftover water, and ensure the unit is dry. We reattach the FCU Cover, and your unit is ready!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

Regular servicing ensures your unit operates efficiently, reducing energy bills and preventing costly major repairs. It also improves air quality by removing dust and mold.

For residential units used daily, we recommend servicing every 3 months. For units used less frequently, every 6 months is sufficient.

Water leakage is often caused by a blocked drainage pipe due to dust and dirt accumulation. A general service or chemical wash can usually resolve this.

A Chemical Wash cleans the coil and parts without dismantling the entire unit. An Overhaul involves fully dismantling the unit for a deep chemical soak of every component.
